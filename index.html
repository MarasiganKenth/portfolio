About me

my recent projects

GETravel

GETravel is a travel advisor application made by my team, with me as the UI designer, using Android Kotlin for our Object Oriented Programming subject. GETravel is an application that allows its users to discover and locate establishments that are within their budget.

Sophie

Sophie is a Japanese Role-Playing Memory game made by my team, with me as the debugger, using Java for our final project in our Java Programming subject. Our Sophie program won the Creative Project Award in the Creative Project Exhibit held by the College of Information Technology Education department.

Le Festin

Le Festin is a website for a restaurant where you can browse their menu, make reservations and contact the business for queries. Le Festin was made with HTML and CSS for our final project in our Human-Computer Interaction subject and is a part of the Creative Project Exhibit of the College of Information Technology Education department.

Sale Poster

A poster for a laptop brand that I made for fun. I scrolled through a tutorial on how to make a gradient sphere which inspired me to create the whole backdrop then decided to make a poster to add to my portfolio.

Girl From Nowhere

A vector art of the famous Thai horror series, Girl From Nowhere. I made this vector art using only Adobe Photoshop. This specific vector art is more detailed compared to my previous works which made it time-comsuming. Nevertheless, the intricate detailing contributes to its overall visual appeal.

Octasia Menu

I was commissioned by Octasia Restaurant, a restaurant in Saudi Arabia that serves Filipino, Thai, and Chinese cuisine, to recreate their menu.
